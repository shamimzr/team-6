The scope of the project

The treasure hunt app is broadly available on all type of smartphones and mobile device featuring a modern, HTML5-compatible web browser. The main goal of the application is to attract users of a younger generation to play individual or in teams to earn scores. The web app is created in such way that is attractive and available to all users and encourage them to play in teams. Each user will have the chance to play full session of the Treasure Hunt as it is made up of multiple modes of questions in which their correct answers will earn them points and wrong answers cost points. In addition to answering the question with the correct answer, players will also need to be in roughly the right place since some questions need location access. As a result, scores will be then viewed on the scoreboard page to show the ranks of players.
