Help

In order to play the Treasure Hunt, you should follow the steps above:

Click on “Lets Get Started” and provide your name. You will receive an input error if your name already exists, and you will need to try with different username.
As you submit your name, you will be directed to select a Treasure Hunt session from the list and view the questions.
On the question page, you are able to see your name, score, and have access to view scoreboard page from the top of your screen.
There are multiple modes of question such as numerical, Boolean, text and multiple-choice. Once you submit your answer you will view a submission alert message.
Skip button is also provided to enable you to skip the question and some questions require to have location access.
Once you completed the quiz, the app will be directed to scoreboard page to show your final score and rank between all other players. Then you can either exit or use “play again” button to be redirected to the Treasure Hunt list page.
